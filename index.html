A brief timeline of Beckham's achievements:

  • 1975: Born May 2 in London, England.
  • 1986: Is the mascot for Manchester United against West Ham.
  • 1989: Signs schoolboy forms for United.
  • 1992: Wins FA Youth Cup and then makes his professional debut.
  • 1996: Makes his England debut in a World Cup qualifier.
  • 1998: Slammed by England fans after being sent off against Argentina at the World Cup.
  • 1999: Marries Spice Girl Victoria Adams and they have children Brooklyn (1999) Romeo (2002), Cruz (2005) and Harper (2011). Man United win the EPL, FA Cup and Champions League.
  • 2000: Made captain of England's national team.
  • 2002: England booted from the World Cup by Brazil.
  • 2003: Joins Real Madrid.
  • 2006: Relinquishes England captaincy after another failed World Cup campaign.
  • 2007: Wins first title with Real, then joins LA Galaxy.
  • 2009-10: Goes on loan to AC Milan.
  • 2012: Wins second MLS cup in final performance for Galaxy, then joins Paris St-Germain on a short contract.
  • 2013: PSG win first League title, Beckham retires and he could not hold back tears during his last football match.

For further information about his life and career, please visit Wikipedia.
